Fun Gameplay

Experience a wrangling puzzle management environment as you guide your cat to stamp all of the right bills at the right time.

Unique Story

Whisker President takes players on a fun adventure filled with campaigns, distractions, cats and more cats. Mysteriously the president became a cat in a suit and now you have to help the cat do what he needs to do without getting him caught and distracted from the job. Discover, experiment, and stamp as you survive through various days of the presidential campaign!

Political Intrigue

Navigate the challenges of presidential life, from rival candidates to scandalous distractions never seen before in the white house.
